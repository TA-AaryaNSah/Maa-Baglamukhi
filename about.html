A Divine Heritage

प्राचीन सिद्ध पीठ माँ बगलामुखी मंदिर

नलखेड़ा स्थित माँ बगलामुखी का यह मंदिर महाभारत कालीन है। किवदंतियों के अनुसार, पांडवों ने श्री कृष्ण के निर्देश पर महाभारत युद्ध जीतने के लिए यहाँ माँ बगलामुखी की आराधना की थी। यह मंदिर भारत के प्रमुख तांत्रिक पीठों में से एक माना जाता है।

The idol of Maa Baglamukhi here is 'Swayambhu' (self-manifested) and holds immense power. Devotees from all over the world visit Nalkheda to perform Havan and Pooja to overcome enemies, legal disputes, and life's hurdles.

"माँ बगलामुखी को पीताम्बरा भी कहा जाता है, जो शत्रुओं का नाश करने और भक्तों की रक्षा करने वाली देवी हैं।"

5000+ Years Old

Temple Significance

Why Nalkheda Baglamukhi Mandir is considered one of the most powerful spiritual centers.

Miraculous Havan

The Havan performed here uses Red Chillies (Mirchi Havan) which is unique and believed to destroy all negativity and enemy forces immediately.

Pandava Era

Established by Maharaja Yudhishthira deeply connects this land to the Mahabharata era and Lord Krishna's guidance.

Lakhundar River

Situated on the banks of the pious Lakhundar river, the temple environment is charged with natural spiritual energy.

Sacred Trees

Rare combination of Bel Patra, Champa, and Peepal trees growing together signifies the presence of divine forces.

The Three Powers

Trishakti Virajman

The sanctum sanctorum creates a powerful triangle of energy. Maa Baglamukhi is seated in the center, but she is not alone.

  • Maa Baglamukhi (Center): The main deity, worshiped for power, victory, and protection.
  • Maa Mahalakshmi (Right): Giving wealth and prosperity to the devotees.
  • Maa Saraswati (Left): Bestowing knowledge and wisdom.
